About Aventus
Tattoo Lounge

Aventus Tattoo Lounge was formed in 2020 by Matt and Victoria Egan. We are a Private Tattoo Studio based in North Hykeham in Lincoln.

As a Private Studio, all bookings are by appointment only. This gives you our full attention whilst you are being tattooed and you don’t have to worry about us having to jump up and deal with walk-ins getting priced up, taking away time you are paying for, it also makes the environment a lot more relaxed and comfortable for our clients.

We have a TV and music for you to listen to whilst being tattooed and drinks are on us. Tea, Coffee, energy drinks ect... EVERY tattoo we do comes with a FREE pot of Yayo Familia Aftercare (By far the best available on the market) to ensure tattoos have the best chance of healing perfectly.
